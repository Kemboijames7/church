Who we are

Our Mission

At AIC Fellowship Annex, our mission is to glorify God by fostering a Christ-centered community where people can grow in faith, love, and service.

Brief History & Progress

AIC Fellowship Annex is a new congregation commissioned by AIC Fellowship Eldoret during its local church mission Sunday on March 5, 2017. It began as a small prayer group and was inaugurated as a full Local Church on March 10, 2019, in a grand ceremony presided over by Bishop Rev. Luka Maiyo.

The church started with two services on March 12, 2017, with over 400 believers. Currently, it has four Sunday services, a mid-week service, and other daily spiritual programs. With a vision to be the light of God in our generation, the congregation is committed to obeying the Great Commission (Matthew 28:19-20).

Core Values

  • Spreading the Gospel through biblical teaching and outreach.
  • Strengthening believers by equipping them with the Word of God.
  • Serving the community with compassion and kindness.
  • Encouraging fellowship and unity among all members.

We strive to be a place where everyone feels welcome, valued, and empowered to live out their faith in Jesus Christ.
